Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Mojo Manor ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website mojomanorwaynesville.com or interact with us in connection with a booking.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and any other information you voluntarily provide when filling out a contact form or making an inquiry.
  • Booking Data: Check-in/check-out dates, number of guests, and payment details processed through our booking platform (Lodgify or Airbnb). We do not store full payment card numbers.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs, collected automatically via analytics tools.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to improve your browsing experience and enable analytics. You may control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and manage your booking or inquiry
  • Communicate with you about your reservation, including confirmations and updates
  • Respond to customer service requests and support needs
  • Improve our website content and user experience
  • Send marketing communications (only if you have opted in)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Lodgify — booking and availability management
  • Airbnb — third-party booking platform
  • Google Analytics — website usage analytics
  • Google Maps — embedded location map
  • Pinterest — social media marketing and image sharing
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external sites.

4. Pinterest Data Usage

We use the Pinterest API to share property images and content on Pinterest for marketing purposes. Pinterest may collect usage data in accordance with their Privacy Policy. By using our site and interacting with Pinterest-embedded content, you acknowledge Pinterest's data collection practices.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Types of cookies we may use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the site to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Marketing Cookies: Used by third-party platforms like Pinterest to show you relevant ads

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent. Note that some features of the site may not function properly without cookies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. Booking records are typically retained for 7 years for tax and legal purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@mojomanor.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
